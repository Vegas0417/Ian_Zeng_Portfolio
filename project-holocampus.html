What this prototype demonstrates

Core user goal

Let learners access campus resources and experiences without being blocked by location, time, or physical constraints.

Interaction focus

Show how a multi‑step experience can stay understandable: clear entry point, lightweight guidance, and visible feedback for each action.

Why video

For early concepts, a video prototype communicates the flow, pacing, and UI cues faster than static screens, especially for mixed‑reality interactions.
